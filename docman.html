DocMan


DocMan is a mobile application which records snoring every day and analyses it. It keeps a tab of all your symptoms and creates your personal health journal. DocMan also gives an alert if your condition is deteriorating and will provide a list of appropriate measures to be taken to bring you back to a state of normalcy.It gives the details of the specialists near you whom you can consult. DocMan is both doctor and patient-friendly. Doctors can refer to your health journal to choose a suitable treatment.
